Inkling Summer Picnic 2016

Summary:

The theme this year: the quintessential picnic, complete with a BBQ, pie bake-off, music, and games. To give the picnic a cohesive look, I created a design kit for the event organizers—inspired by classic gingham patterns and BBQ imagery.

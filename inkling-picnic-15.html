Inkling Summer Picnic 2015

Summary:

In 2015, the Inkling Summer Picnic was set to take place at the Shoreline Aquatic Center in Mountain View. We wanted the look and feel of the event to be bright, light, and summery—an idyllic day at the lake. To help make the picnic fun and unforgettable, I helped define the theme and design kit to guide the visual direction of the event.

Deliverables:

  • Illustrations
  • Button swag
  • Invitation
